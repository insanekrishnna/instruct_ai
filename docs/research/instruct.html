Your apps,connected

Instruct doesn't just read your data; it acts on it. Securely connect your tools, and it will pull context from one app to execute work in another, making your software work together instantly.

Just describe the work

Tell Instruct what you need, and it gets it done. Whether you need it to write, edit, fetch data, or generate documents, it handles the manual execution across your apps exactly as requested.

Automate

Turn tasksinto workflows

Any successful task can become a permanent process. Once Instruct completes a job, simply ask it to run on a schedule. It turns your one-off request into a recurring workflow instantly.

Fluent in every workflow.

Instruct adapts to the unique needs of every department. From managing pipelines to filtering candidates, it executes the specific tasks that keep your business moving.
